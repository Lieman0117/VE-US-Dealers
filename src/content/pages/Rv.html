Specs for RV Batteries

Our 12V RV and boat lithium batteries are built with premium A grade cells, providing dependable performance for long term use with an expected cycle life of 2,000 to 5,000 cycles. These units are suitable for RV power systems, boats, marine applications, trolling motors, fish finders, golf carts, and off grid electrical setups. Dealers can supply these as direct replacements for lead acid batteries while giving customers a meaningful upgrade in performance and longevity.

Each unit is engineered for durability in harsh conditions with an IP65 waterproof rating that protects the battery in a wide range of weather environments. This makes installation suitable for both road and marine use.

These batteries provide a strong balance of power output, long life, and reliability, giving dealers a versatile product for a wide variety of customer needs. Every unit is supported by a 5 year quality warranty from the date of purchase.
