Why does VE/US use Zelle?

Zelle is owned by a group of the largest U.S. banks. The cash transfer never leaves the banking system. Because it is safe and controlled, the banks provide this as a FREE cash payment system.

This minimizes another VE/US supply cost. Dealers need maximum retail markup to cover all local retail costs.

VE/US’s goal is to eliminate all unnecessary costs for providing Dealers the best value-engineered products currently available to local service and parts Dealers.
