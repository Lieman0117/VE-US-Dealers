Specs for Golf Cart Batteries

Our 48V lithium golf cart batteries use brand new Grade A cells with full UL certification, providing consistent performance and dependable durability. Each unit includes an upgraded 200A smart BMS that supports continuous discharge currents up to 200 amps, with peak outputs of 1000 amps for 3 to 5 seconds, 300 amps for 32 seconds, and 600 amps for 32 seconds. This prevents performance cut offs and ensures stable operation for demanding golf cart applications.

With a total energy capacity of 5.12 kWh, these 48V 100Ah batteries have been tested to power a golf cart with a 3 kW motor for up to 30 miles per charge. Dealers can also monitor system performance through the integrated Bluetooth smart BMS, allowing real time data access through the manufacturer app.
