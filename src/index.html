Vehicle Electronics Us

Vehicle Electronics (VE/US) supplies Dealers with Value Engineered lithium products designed to upgrade their customers’ existing machines at prices competitive with current Lead Acid replacements. We do not sell directly to customers. Instead, we support qualified local Service Dealers — the Tech Experts who handle installation, service, and customer care. VE/US provides efficient, direct manufacturer delivery and simple Special Order supply so Dealers can offer modern lithium solutions without unnecessary costs or middlemen.
